About Me

I'm Ida — a curious 12-year-old who loves making things. I'm learning web development, building interactive tools and games, and figuring out how to bring ideas to life with code.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually reading, playing sports, or thinking about my next project. I like experimenting with new ideas and seeing what happens when I try something I haven't done before.

I believe the best way to learn something is to just start building it. Every project on this site started as a small idea that turned into something I wanted to share.
