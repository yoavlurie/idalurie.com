About Me

I'm Ida — a curious 12-year-old who loves making things. I'm learning web development, designing games, and figuring out how to bring ideas to life with code.

I'm drawn to building things that people can actually use and interact with. Whether it's a small game, a website, or a creative tool, I like starting with a simple idea and seeing where it goes.

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually drawing, taking photos, or thinking about my next project. I believe the best way to learn something is to just start building it.
